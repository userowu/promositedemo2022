Nessa is a fictional character from the Pokemon franchise. She is a water gym leader, model, and pokemon lover. Nessa appears in Pokemon Sword and Shield, Masters, Twilight Wings, and Adventures. Nessa is multitalented, wielding skills in Pokemon battles, managing a Pokemon stadium, swimming, and modeling. From Galar, she is one of the most famous icons. Not to mention, her personality is one of composure and level-headedness. Nessa never backs down from a challenge, whether it is swimming or Pokemon battling.
